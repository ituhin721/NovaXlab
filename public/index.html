Featured Assets

NOVA (NovaToken)

NOVA powers the NovaX ecosystem, enabling fee-less transactions, premium access, governance roles, and staking capabilities. With deflationary design and utility expansion plans, NOVA is our flagship asset.

LYRA (LyraCoin)

LYRA is the governance token that empowers users to shape NovaX’s protocol through voting, proposals, and treasury decisions. It rewards participation and trust in our decentralized journey.
